## 1. Introduction

This Platform Policy outlines the rules, standards, and enforcement mechanisms that govern all users of our platform. Our goal is to maintain a high-quality, trustworthy environment where valuable content is prioritized and misinformation is prevented.

By using our platform, you agree to comply with this policy. Violations may result in content removal, Kurral Score penalties, loss of monetization eligibility, or account suspension.

---

## 2. Content Standards

### 2.1 Factual Accuracy

**Requirement:** All posts must contain accurate, verifiable information.

**Enforcement:**
- Every post is automatically fact-checked in real-time using AI agents
- Claims are extracted and verified against trusted sources including government sites, academic institutions, and established news organizations
- Posts with false claims verified with high confidence (>70%) are immediately blocked
- High-risk claims in health, finance, or politics that cannot be verified are flagged for human review

**High-Risk Domains:**
- Health and medical information
- Financial advice and investment information
- Political claims and election information

**Status Classifications:**
- **Clean:** All claims verified or low risk
- **Needs Review:** Unverified high-risk claims or claims with mixed evidence
- **Blocked:** False claims verified with high confidence

### 2.2 Value Creation

**Requirement:** Content must provide genuine value to the community.

**Evaluation:**
- All posts are scored using our proprietary Kurral Score system (0-100)
- Scores are calculated dynamically using AI analysis
- Content is evaluated based on discussion quality, fact-check results, and community trust
- Low-value or spam content receives lower scores and reduced visibility

### 2.3 Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited:

**Misinformation:**
- False claims verified with high confidence
- Deliberately misleading information
- Conspiracy theories without credible evidence
- Medical misinformation that could cause harm

**Hate Speech and Harassment:**
- Content targeting individuals or groups based on protected characteristics
- Threats, intimidation, or incitement to violence
- Bullying or harassment of other users

**Spam and Low-Quality Content:**
- Repetitive, automated, or low-effort posts
- Content designed solely to manipulate algorithms
- Excessive self-promotion or advertising
- Duplicate content across multiple posts

**Illegal Content:**
- Content that violates applicable laws
- Copyright infringement
- Defamation or libel
- Content promoting illegal activities

## 4. Monetization Policy

### 4.1 Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for monetization, users must meet all of the following criteria:

**Kurral Score Requirement:**
- Maintain a Kurral Score of 77 or higher
- Scores are calculated dynamically and update in real-time

**Account Age Requirement:**
- Account must be at least 30 days old

**Violation History:**
- No severe violations in the last 90 days
- No repeated policy violations

### 4.2 Payment Calculation

**Payment Structure:**
- Payments are based on the value score of each post
- Higher value scores result in higher payments
- Payments are calculated at day 7 after post creation
- Adjustments may be made at day 14 and day 30 for posts with continued engagement

**Payment Processing:**
- Payments are processed 14 days after post creation
- Base payment is determined at day 7
- Additional payments may be issued for posts with significant engagement increases

### 4.3 Eligibility Maintenance

**Ongoing Requirements:**
- Maintain Kurral Score above 77
- Continue creating valuable, fact-checked content
- Avoid policy violations

**Loss of Eligibility:**
- Kurral Score drops below 77
- Severe policy violation
- Multiple violations within 90 days
- Suspended accounts are ineligible

**Recovery:**
- Users can regain eligibility by improving their Kurral Score to 77+ and maintaining it for 30 days
- Violations decay over time (50% impact after 30 days, 0% after 365 days)
- Consistent quality contributions help recover scores

## 5. Kurral Score System

### 5.1 Score Range

- **Range:** 0-100
- **Starting Score:** 65 (for new users)
- **Excellent Tier:** 88-100
- **Good Tier:** 77-87 (monetization eligible)
- **Fair Tier:** 65-76
- **Poor Tier:** 53-64
- **Very Poor Tier:** 0-52

### 5.2 Score Components

Scores are calculated using our proprietary algorithm that evaluates:
- Content quality history
- Violation history
- Engagement quality
- Consistency of contributions
- Community trust

### 5.3 Score Updates

**Real-Time Updates:**
- When posts are published and fact-checked
- When policy violations are detected
- When posts are blocked or flagged

**Batch Updates:**
- Full recalculation every 24 hours
- Time decay applied to violations
- Score history maintained

### 5.4 Score Impact

**Positive Factors:**
- High-quality, fact-checked content
- Verified true claims
- Constructive discussions
- Consistent activity
- No policy violations

**Negative Factors:**
- False claims verified with high confidence
- Policy violations (blocked posts)
- High-risk unverified claims
- Spam or low-quality content
- Repeated violations

## 6. Violations and Enforcement

### 6.1 Violation Types

**Severe Violations:**
- False claims in high-risk domains (health, finance, politics) verified with high confidence
- Hate speech or harassment
- Multiple false claims in a single post
- Deliberate misinformation

**Moderate Violations:**
- Unverified high-risk claims
- Low-quality or spam content
- Policy violations requiring review
- Repeated low-value content

**Minor Violations:**
- Unverified claims in low-risk domains
- Minor policy infractions
- Low engagement quality

### 6.2 Enforcement Actions

**Automatic Actions:**
- **Blocked:** Posts with false claims (high confidence) are immediately blocked
- **Flagged for Review:** High-risk unverified claims are flagged for human review
- **Score Penalties:** Violations result in Kurral Score reductions
- **Visibility Reduction:** Low-quality content receives reduced visibility

**Account Actions:**
- **Warning:** First-time minor violations
- **Temporary Suspension:** Repeated violations or moderate violations
- **Permanent Ban:** Severe violations or persistent policy violations
- **Monetization Suspension:** Score drops below 77 or violation history

### 6.3 Score Penalties

**Penalty Structure:**
- Blocked post: Significant score reduction
- Needs review: Moderate score reduction
- False claims: Score reduction based on confidence and domain risk
- Repeated violations: Cumulative penalties

**Recovery:**
- Violations decay over time (50% impact after 30 days, 0% after 365 days)
- Consistent quality contributions help recover scores
- No new violations for 90 days improves trust component

## 7. Appeals Process

### 7.1 Appeal Eligibility

Users may appeal:
- Post blocks or removals
- Policy decisions
- Score penalties
- Account suspensions

### 7.2 Appeal Process

**Submission:**
- Appeals must be submitted within 30 days of the action
- Include relevant evidence and context
- Explain why the decision should be reconsidered

**Review:**
- Appeals are reviewed by human moderators
- Review considers original policy decision, user history, and new evidence
- Decisions are made within 14 business days

**Outcomes:**
- Appeal granted: Action reversed, score restored if applicable
- Appeal denied: Original decision stands, explanation provided
- Partial appeal: Modified decision based on new information

### 7.3 Final Review

For severe violations or account-level actions, users may request a final review by platform administrators.
