## 4. Data Ownership and Control

### 4.1 You Own Your Data

**You own all of your data on Kural.** This includes:
- Your profile information
- Your posts and comments
- Your algorithm preferences and feed settings
- Your audience targeting settings
- Your engagement data
- Your Kural Score and value statistics

### 4.2 Data Export

You have the right to export your data at any time. To request a copy of your data, please email us at **support@kurral.app** with "Data Export Request" in the subject line. We will provide your data in a standard, machine-readable format (typically JSON) within 30 days of your request.

Your exported data will include:
- Your profile information and account settings
- All your posts and comments
- Your bookmarks and following list
- Your Kural Score and value statistics
- Your notifications and preferences
- Links to images you've uploaded (image files remain in our storage)

We will verify your identity before providing the export to ensure your data is only shared with you.

### 4.3 Data Deletion

You can delete your account and data at any time. When you delete your account:
- We will delete your personal information from our active systems
- Some information may remain in backup systems for a limited time (typically up to 90 days)
- Content you've shared publicly may remain visible if it has been reposted or shared by others
- We may retain certain information as required by law or for safety purposes

### 4.4 Transparency

We are committed to transparency about how your data is used:
- Every recommendation is explained
- You can see why content appears in your feed
- You have full control over your algorithm preferences
- You can request a copy of all data we have about you at any time
